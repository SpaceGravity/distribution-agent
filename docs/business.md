# CostTracker

## One-line Description
An open-source tool that gives you per-customer cost visibility so you can understand exactly how much each customer costs you to serve.

## Problem Being Solved
Agents companies, agencies, and dev teams struggle to track per-customer costs across infrastructure (cloud compute, storage, APIs, third-party services). Most tools only show aggregate spend, making it impossible to identify unprofitable customers, optimize pricing, or catch runaway costs early. Teams end up using spreadsheets or guessing, which leads to margin erosion and pricing mistakes.

## Target Audience
- SaaS founders and operators who need to understand unit economics per customer
- DevOps and platform engineers tracking multi-tenant infrastructure costs
- Agency owners managing per-client project profitability
- Finance teams at startups needing granular agents cost allocation
- Solo developers building multi-tenant apps who want cost transparency

## Key Features
- Per-customer cost tracking across all infrastructure and services
- Real-time cost dashboard with per-client breakdowns
- Open-source core with a hosted version available (the hosting is a paid product)
- Integrates with major cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure) and third-party APIs
- Automated margin and profitability calculations per customer
- Alerts for cost anomalies and runaway spending

## Pricing Model
Open-source core (free, self-hosted). Hosted version with a managed dashboard starting at $19/month.

## Differentiators
Unlike generic cost monitoring tools (AWS Cost Explorer, Datadog), CostTracker is built specifically for per-customer cost allocation. It connects directly to your billing data and maps costs to individual customers, not just services or accounts. The open-source model means full transparency and no vendor lock-in.
